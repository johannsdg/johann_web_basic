Welcome to Johann!

Johann is a lightweight and flexible “scenario orchestrator”.
It makes it easy to coordinate the actions of groups of computers into cohesive, reconfigurable scenarios.
It’s sort of like the conductor for an orchestra of computers, and you get to write the music.
For more information, please visit https://github.com/johannsdg/johann.
